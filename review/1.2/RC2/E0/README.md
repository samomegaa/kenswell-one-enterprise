# Release 1.2 RC2-E0

## Staffology Pay Options Contract Discovery

RC2-E0 discovers the Staffology Pay Options information currently
available through the Kenswell One runtime.

## Scope

- Pay Options source inventory
- Regular Pay field aliases
- Safe response-shape inspection
- Candidate field-path discovery
- Pay Options coverage catalogue
- Modular verification

## Pay Options domains

- Regular Pay
- Additions & Deductions
- Loans
- Tax & NI
- Other
- Benefits

## Boundary

RC2-E0 introduces no frontend workspace changes and no Staffology
write operations.

Employee values captured during discovery must remain temporary and
must not be committed.
